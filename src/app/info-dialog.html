What is this thing?

Well, this is a monument to our very own Annie Russo of the eponymous Big Balls Annie fame. She loves Animal Crossing, and while I can't build a whole Animal Crossing themed experience, I can build a playground inspired by Annie and her deep, unrelenting love of anthropomorphic animal friends.

Thanks to Allie Danneker for excellent photographic contributions. This was built without Annie's knowledge, permission, or consent. I'm not sorry.

How things work!

The Stonk Market is a very primitive approximation of a market, where changes in prices are random about every second or so.

The Math Corner is a simple generator that self-checks a random math problem. To make this doable by a human without a calculator, these are limited to values 1-10 and only do addition, subtraction, and multiplication to keep values reasonable.

Nerd stuff!

This is a standard Angular 10 app and all computation is done on the client side. Hosted on AWS with Cloudfront and Code Pipelines. Our whimsical font is the delightful "Comic Neue" and the favicon is custom designed by me.
